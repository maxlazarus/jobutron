Job Description:

A co-op position is available for the summer of 2015 in the research group of Professor David Wilkinson in the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering. The project involves development of advanced materials, electrode structures, and electrochemical cells for low cost, environmentally friendly energy storage devices. The co-op candidate will be directly involved in these aspects of the research and will closely work with a research associate in the lab. The student will learn very relevant electrochemical skills (potentiostatic/galvanostatic and impedance measurement), materials characterizations (X-ray diffraction, Scanning Electron Microscope), electrode preparation and cell assembly. This is an industrially-supported project. Supervision and guidance will be provided on a daily basis by a research associate and graduate students, who are engaged in this project.


Job Requirements:

The position is hands-on and requires general knowledge of laboratory work and a high interest in performing experimental research in the lab.
Students are highly encouraged to apply for an NSERC USRA scholarship. Further information about NSERC USRA scholarships can be found at: http://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/UG-PC/USRA-BRPC_eng.asp
